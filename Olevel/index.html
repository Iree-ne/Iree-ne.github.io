Welcome to Marian!

Marian Girls High School is located in Bagamoyo at Majengo area in Tanzania.

The school was started officially in 1997 with registration number as Marian Girls High School.

We Lead by example, embracing change and striving for excellence We believe that learning should be vibrant and fun and that time spent at School should be a rewarding experience. Therefore we are committed to a programme of enrichment for learners that involve a variety of events and opportunities.

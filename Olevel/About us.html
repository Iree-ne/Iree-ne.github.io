About us

Marian Girl's high school was established in 1997 by the Holy Ghost father's(E.a.Province) in Bagamoyo ancient town.The place where the school is situated was formerly known as Marian free village in the 18th century.

Marian Girl's high school was built under the guidance and management of the founder ,Fr. Valentine Bayo CSSp.The need for establishing a girl's school was aimed at the provision of quality education to girls especially those living in the coastal area.

Marian girl's high school offers ordinary level education and advanced level education.It admits students mostly with great performance in the primary level for ordinary level studies and division 1 1nd 2 for advanced level studies.Marian curriculum is based on Australian Curriculum.

It has almost 65 staff members both teaching and non teaching staff.It teaches subjects like ENGLISH,MATHEMATICS,ARTS,SCIENCE.

Our mision.

The schools are striving to compete,the role of parents,family and community in leading the girls in their care to a great fidelity to God , a sense of justice to all,and love for a neighbor that will form responsible leaders for the future.
